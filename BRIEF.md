# BRIEF OF THE COMPANY PROJECT

# Which company did you choose?

I choose a printing and designing solutions company that is based in Johannesburg.
The company’s current website is : https://www.speedyclearprint.co.za

# How did you find them?

I have personally worked with the company before doing deliveries for them.

# Why did you choose this company specifically?

The company is also deals with design solutions, so I knew that it might be hard to meet the standard but I wanted a challenge to see how much and how far I can put my skills to the test.

# Why does their website need a redesign?

The first thing I focused on was the overall look and feel. The old site felt fairly traditional, with information laid out in a way that didn't really guide the eye anywhere in particular. For the redesign, I leaned into an editorial-style aesthetic — warm paper-like backgrounds, refined typography, generous white space, and a cohesive colour palette throughout. It gives the site a more polished, professional feel that I think genuinely reflects the quality of the printing work we do.
Navigation was something I really wanted to simplify as it was difficult for one to do so. I added a sticky nav bar with smooth scrolling, so people can jump straight to About, Services, Capabilities, Clients, or Contact without ever losing their place on the page. It sounds like a small thing, but it makes browsing feel a lot faster and more intuitive.
Customer reach/way to contact was something I thought could be improved. I also put real effort into improving how customers can reach them. I added stronger call-to-action buttons, a working quote request form, click-to-call functionality, and a floating WhatsApp button. Now, if someone wants a quote, they can just tap the WhatsApp icon and send a message right away instead of hunting around for a phone number or email address.
Not that this was a problem but it could be improved so I made sure everything is fully responsive — the layout, hover interactions, and spacing all adapt smoothly across screen sizes. The result is a site that feels consistent no matter how someone accesses it, and one that I think finally does justice to Speedyclearprint's professionalism and the quality they offer.
